Case sensitive lexical tokens:
  One-line comments starting with // 
  Multiple-line comments starting with /* and ending with */
  Identifiers starting with a letter or _ and followed by letter, digit or _
  Integer literals without sign  
  Real literals with floating point
	12.3, 2., .34
  Real literals with mantissa and exponent
	34.12E-3, 3e3, 2e+5
  Char literals between ''
  Char literals identifying the ASCII code, e.g. '\126'
  The two special char constants '\n' and '\t'
